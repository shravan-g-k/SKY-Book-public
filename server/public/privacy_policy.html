SKY Book Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect:

  • a. User-Generated Content: SKY Book allows you to create books and pages to store your personal content, such as text, images, and multimedia.
  • b. Chat with SKY: Conversations with our AI companion, SKY, are stored securely for your convenience. These conversations can be added to your diary by your request.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • Diary Functionality: Your user-generated content, including diary entries, is stored securely within the app for your access and use.

3. Information Security:

  • a. Encryption: We employ industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data. All user-generated content and conversations with SKY are encrypted to ensure privacy.
  • b. Data Access Control: Your diary entries and conversations are accessible only to you. SKY Book offers the option to lock each book individually for added security.

4. Data Sharing:

  • a. Third Parties: We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • b. SKY Conversations: Conversations with SKY can be added to your diary by your request. These conversations are stored securely within the app and are not shared unless you choose to do so.

5. Access and Control:

Data Access: You have the right to access, modify, or delete your user-generated content and conversations at any time.
